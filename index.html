HOT or COLD

What do I do?

This is a Number guessing game called "Hot & Cold". The game goes like this:

  1. I pick a random secret number between 1 to 100 and keep it hidden.
  2. You need to guess until you correctly guess the secret number.
  3. You will get feedback on how close ("hot") or far ("cold") your guess is.
